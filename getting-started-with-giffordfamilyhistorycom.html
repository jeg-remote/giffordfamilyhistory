Visit the Sources page to browse these and other collections, where additional stories and letters can be found.

Technical Details

GiffordFamilyHistory.com is a self-hosted private instance of Gramps Web, an open-source genealogy platform that resides on a portable solid-state removable drive. The current custodian-administrator is John Gifford. If you are interested in becoming an administrator, please email admin@giffordfamilyhistory.com.

Getting Started

 The site works on any browser. The best experience will be on a larger screen such as a desktop computer, laptop, or tablet.

Go to GiffordFamilyHistory.com

 Click on Launch Family Tree button.

Complete a one-time registration by clicking on the Register new account button.

* Important: The username and password are BOTH case-sensitive *
